## Recommended Books for Deep Dives

1. **Goldgar, Anne.** *Tulipmania* (2007) — the revisionist account of the Dutch tulip bubble
2. **Tainter, Joseph.** *The Collapse of Complex Societies* (1988) — framework for understanding civilizational decline
3. **Reinhart, Carmen & Rogoff, Kenneth.** *This Time Is Different: Eight Centuries of Financial Folly* (2009) — sovereign debt crises across 800 years
4. **Bernstein, Peter.** *The Power of Gold* (2000) — gold's role as money across civilizations
5. **Ferguson, Niall.** *The Ascent of Money* (2008) — financial system evolution and crises
6. **Kindleberger, Charles.** *Manias, Panics, and Crashes* (1978, multiple editions) — the classic on financial crises
7. **Diamond, Jared.** *Collapse* (2005) — environmental and societal collapse patterns
