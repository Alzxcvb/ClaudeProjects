## Key Ratios and Comparisons

**2008 GFC Asset Comparison (peak-to-trough or recession period):**
- S&P 500: -57%
- Farmland: +30%
- Gold: +25% (recession), +166% (through 2011)
- Art: -27%
- Oil: -53%
- US Treasuries: positive (negative beta -0.09 to -0.11)

**Defensive vs. Cyclical Equities in Recession:**
- Defensive sectors (staples, healthcare, utilities): ~-30% to -31%
- Cyclical sectors: -50%+ in severe recessions
- Cyclicals 2008-2020 CAGR: -9%
- Cyclicals 2020-2024 CAGR: +77% (recovery)

**Roman Currency Debasement Timeline:**
- 64 AD (Nero): First debasement begins
- 200 AD: Denarius at ~50% silver
- 265 AD: Denarius at 0.5% silver
- 312 AD: Constantine introduces gold Solidus — holds stable 700 years
- 330 AD: 1 Solidus = hundreds of thousands of Denarii

## Quick Reference: What Beats What

**In inflation:** Gold > Commodities > Farmland > Equities > Cash > Bonds

**In deflation:** Bonds > Cash > Gold > Defensive equities > Commodities > Cyclical equities

**In currency collapse:** Hard currency > Gold > Real assets > Skills > Everything in dying currency

**In bubble pop:** Cash > Fundamentally sound equities > Gold > Bubble asset

**In state collapse:** Gold > Productive land > Foreign assets > Military power > Government debt
