Legs Day

Quads • Hamstrings • Calves • Core

Wednesday (Legs A) & Saturday (Legs B)

Exercises

01
Barbell Back Squat
4 × 8
Foundation movement. The king of all exercises for total body strength and muscle.
Form Cues
  • Bar on upper traps (high bar) or rear delts (low bar)
  • Feet shoulder-width or slightly wider, toes out 15-30°
  • Break at hips and knees simultaneously
  • Descend until hip crease is below knee (parallel or deeper)
  • Chest up, core braced, drive through whole foot
  • Knees track over toes — don't let them cave in
02
Romanian Deadlift
3 × 10
Hamstrings and glutes. The best posterior chain builder.
Form Cues
  • Start standing with bar at hips, slight knee bend
  • Hinge at hips, push butt back
  • Bar stays close to legs — slides down thighs
  • Go until you feel a deep hamstring stretch (mid-shin typically)
  • Flat back throughout — stop before your back rounds
  • Squeeze glutes hard at top
03
Leg Press
3 × 12
Quad volume. Safe way to push heavy without spinal load.
Form Cues
  • Feet shoulder-width on platform, mid-height
  • Lower until knees reach ~90°
  • Don't lock out fully at top — keep tension
  • Keep lower back pressed into pad (no butt wink)
  • Higher foot placement = more glute/ham; lower = more quad
04
Leg Curl (Machine)
3 × 12
Hamstring isolation. Lying or seated — whatever machine is available.
Form Cues
  • Pad sits just above heel
  • Curl all the way up, squeeze at top
  • Slow eccentric (3 sec down)
  • Don't lift hips off pad
05
Calf Raises
4 × 15
Calves need high volume. Standing or seated, use whatever machine is available.
Form Cues
  • Full range: stretch at bottom, squeeze at top
  • 2-sec pause at top of each rep
  • Slow, controlled — no bouncing
  • Standing hits gastrocnemius; seated hits soleus (do both if time)

Notes

Both days: Add core workout after — see Abs page for Workout C (Strength + Isometric).

Rest 2-3 minutes on squats and RDLs. Rest 60-90 seconds on leg press, curls, and calves.

Total session time: ~60-75 minutes including core.
