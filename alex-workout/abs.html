Abs & Core

Three Rotating Workouts • 15-20 min each

Schedule

Day Workout Focus
Monday (after Push A) Workout A Stability + Flexion
Wednesday (after Legs A) Workout C Strength + Isometric
Friday (after Pull B) Workout B Lateral + Rotation
Saturday (after Legs B) Workout C Strength + Isometric

Workout A — Stability + Flexion

Monday after Push A

01
Dead Bug
3 × 10/side
Core stability foundation. Teaches anti-extension.
Form Cues
  • Lie on back, arms straight up, knees at 90°
  • Press lower back into floor — maintain throughout
  • Extend opposite arm + leg simultaneously
  • Exhale as you extend, inhale to return
  • If lower back lifts off floor, reduce range of motion
02
Reverse Crunch
3 × 15
Lower abs. Better than regular crunches for abs visibility.
Form Cues
  • Lie on back, hands by sides or gripping bench
  • Knees bent, lift hips off the ground
  • Curl pelvis toward ribcage — not just lifting legs
  • Slow and controlled — no momentum
03
Bicycle Crunch (Slow, 3-sec tempo)
3 × 20 total
Obliques + rectus abdominis. The slow tempo is key — no rushing.
Form Cues
  • Hands behind head, don't pull on neck
  • Rotate torso to bring elbow to opposite knee
  • 3-second hold at peak contraction
  • Fully extend the non-working leg

Workout B — Lateral + Rotation

Friday after Pull B

01
Plank Shoulder Taps
3 × 10/side
Anti-rotation under load. Keep hips completely still.
Form Cues
  • High plank position, feet wider than normal for stability
  • Tap opposite shoulder without rotating hips
  • Brace core hard before each tap
  • Slow and deliberate — no rocking
02
Side Plank Hip Dips
3 × 12/side
Oblique strength. Builds the tapered waist look.
Form Cues
  • Side plank on forearm, body in straight line
  • Dip hip toward floor, then drive up above neutral
  • Control the movement — don't collapse
  • Stack feet or stagger for balance
03
Russian Twist (Bodyweight)
3 × 12/side
Rotational core strength. Bodyweight only — add weight later.
Form Cues
  • Sit with knees bent, lean back 45°
  • Feet off the ground (or on ground if too hard)
  • Rotate torso side to side, touch floor each side
  • Move from the core, not just the arms

Workout C — Strength + Isometric

Wednesday & Saturday after Legs

01
Hollow Body Hold
3 × 20-30 sec
Full core activation. The gymnast's secret weapon for visible abs.
Form Cues
  • Lie on back, arms overhead, legs straight
  • Press lower back into floor
  • Lift shoulders and legs off ground simultaneously
  • Body forms a shallow "banana" shape
  • If too hard: bend knees, or arms by sides
  • Breathe normally — don't hold breath
02
Lying Leg Raises
3 × 12
Lower abs under load. Can also do hanging if available.
Form Cues
  • Lie flat, hands under glutes or gripping bench
  • Legs straight, raise to 90°
  • Lower slowly (3-4 sec) — this is where the work happens
  • Don't let feet touch the ground between reps
  • Keep lower back pressed down throughout
