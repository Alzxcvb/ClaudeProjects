Pull Day

Back • Biceps

Tuesday (Pull A) & Friday (Pull B + Abs)

Exercises

01
Pull-ups or Lat Pulldown
4 × 8
Back width — the V-taper. Start with lat pulldown if you can't do 8 pull-ups yet.
Form Cues
  • Grip just outside shoulder width
  • Initiate by pulling shoulder blades down and back
  • Pull to upper chest (not behind neck)
  • Full dead hang at bottom — no half reps
  • If doing pulldown: lean back slightly, pull to collarbone
02
Barbell Row (or Dumbbell Row)
4 × 8
Back thickness. Barbell for load, dumbbells for better range of motion.
Form Cues
  • Hinge at hips ~45 degrees, flat back
  • Pull to lower chest / upper belly
  • Squeeze shoulder blades at top, 1-sec hold
  • Don't jerk — control both directions
  • DB row: one knee on bench, pull to hip
03
Face Pulls
3 × 15
Rear delts + rotator cuff health. Non-negotiable for shoulder longevity.
Form Cues
  • Cable at face height, rope attachment
  • Pull to face, split the rope to ears
  • Externally rotate at end — thumbs point back
  • Keep elbows high, squeeze rear delts
  • Light weight, high reps — this is a health exercise
04
Dumbbell Bicep Curl
3 × 12
Bicep size. Alternate arms or both together.
Form Cues
  • Elbows pinned to sides — no swinging
  • Full supination at top (pinky rotates up)
  • Slow negative (3 sec down)
  • Full extension at bottom — no half reps
05
Hammer Curl
3 × 12
Brachialis + forearm. Neutral grip (palms facing each other).
Form Cues
  • Palms face each other throughout
  • Same rules as bicep curl: no swing, slow negative
  • Can do cross-body hammers for brachialis emphasis

Notes

Friday (Pull B): Add abs workout after — see Abs page for Workout B (Lateral + Rotation).

Rest 2-3 minutes on pull-ups/rows. Rest 60-90 seconds on face pulls and curls.

Total session time: ~50-65 minutes including abs.
